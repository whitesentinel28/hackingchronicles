> 🔒 **Educational cybersecurity podcast series telling legendary hacking stories through cinematic storytelling**

Transform complex cybersecurity incidents into thrilling audio dramas that anyone can understand and enjoy. From the Morris Worm to modern AI-powered attacks, we explore the human stories behind the code.

---

## 🌟 **What We Do**

**Hacking Chronicles** brings you the most fascinating cybersecurity stories in history through:
- 🎭 **Cinematic storytelling** with Alex (narrator) and White Sentinel (expert)
- 🌍 **5 languages**: Korean, English, Spanish, Portuguese, Japanese
- 🎥 **Multi-platform**: Video podcasts, audio-only versions, and social shorts
- 📚 **Educational focus**: Learning through legendary incidents
